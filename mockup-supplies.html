What's on screen

  • Six categories: fuel, consumables, disposables, toiletries, event, other. Empty categories are hidden (no "Event" row on a camping trip).
  • Durable gear is NOT here — tents, stoves, chairs, sleeping bags live on the Packing tab under a new Gear view. Supplies = bought/consumed for this trip only.
  • Three statuses — red dot (Needed), amber (Claimed), green (Purchased). Claim toggles Needed → Claimed; buyer marks it Purchased.
  • One claimer per item (v1). "Everyone bring your own X" lives in v2.
  • Where notes land — finalizing a supply-type note creates a row here with `source_note_id` → back to the note.
