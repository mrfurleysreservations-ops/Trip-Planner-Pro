Open questions I'd love your take on

A. Placement of the reuse row. I put it below + Add Item so the "this outfit's current state" stays visually primary. An alternative: pin the reuse row to the top (under the timeline) so it's the first thing you reach for. Phone thumbs are at the bottom though, so lower = easier.

B. Category filter chip ("All 8 ▾"). On a 10-day trip your pool could grow to 30+ items. The dropdown could filter by category (Tops / Bottoms / Shoes / Accessories) so scrolling stays manageable. Needed now or later?

C. Sort. Most-reused first means white shoes rise to the top fast. Alternatives: alphabetical (predictable), most-recently-added (good for new items), by category (grouped). Most-reused is my default.

D. Typeahead vs. chip row. Both cover the same ground but serve different muscle memory: typeahead = "I know what I want, let me type a few letters." Chip row = "remind me what I've got." I'd ship both since they share the same data source.
