The exact pill spec (canonical)

Container:
  • display: inline-flex
  • background: th.card (≈ rgba(0,0,0,0.03))
  • border: 1.5px solid th.cardBorder
  • borderRadius: 20
Button (inactive):
  • background: transparent
  • color: th.muted
  • fontWeight: 500
  • padding: 8px 18px
  • fontSize: 13
  • fontFamily: 'DM Sans', sans-serif
  • borderRadius: 20
Button (active):
  • background: th.accent
  • color: #fff
  • fontWeight: 700
  • everything else same as inactive

What stays the same

Every tab's pill options, labels, emoji, and onClick handlers stay exactly as they are today. Only the container + button inline-styles change. Functionality is untouched — you could swap just the styling and every pill would still filter/route correctly.

What's NOT in this change

Secondary chip rows (Notes' All/Ideas/Finalized, Expenses' category chips, Itinerary's day picker) are a different control and are NOT affected. They keep their own styles. Only the primary pill toggles get unified.
