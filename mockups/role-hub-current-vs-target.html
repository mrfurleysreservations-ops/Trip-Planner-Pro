Role Hub — Current vs Target

What you see today (left) vs what the spec says you should see (right)

TL;DR — what's broken

The Role Picker + sub-nav reorder + role hero all shipped, but the hub hero is still only a generic gradient banner. No expenses data lands on the hub for any role, and Just Here users get redirected straight to /expenses, so they never even see the hub — which is the opposite of what the mockup showed (big "You owe" card on the hub itself).

  • 🔥 All In — hub shows generic "X events coming up" hero. No expense summary, no packing progress, no chat preview. Bookings list is the same for every role.
  • 🙌 Helping Out — hero says "Pick up a task" but lists nothing real (no unclaimed meals, no unassigned packing). No expense summary.
  • 🎟️ Just Here — redirected away from the hub before seeing anything. Lands on the full expenses tab instead of the big "You owe Dan $X · Pay" card that was in the mockup.
  • ✌️ Vibes Only — next-event card works. No "You owe" card next to it. Everything else is same-as-host.

The fix is (a) stop redirecting Just Here away from the hub, (b) fetch expenses/packing/chat summaries in app/trip/[id]/page.tsx, (c) expand each role hero to render a real summary card from that data.
