What stays consistent across both cards

  • Time-of-day gradient band — identical color, height, typography, weather chip placement.
  • Meta row — dress-code pill on the left, contextual counts on the right (events+items packed for groups; events+items count for outfits).
  • Timeline event rows — same dot/line treatment, same icon-prefixed title, same sub-line and description style. The outfit card shows the exact same events as the group card it came from.
  • Card shell — same 16px radius, same border, same shadow.

What's added to the outfit card

  • Inspo CTA + saved inspo — sit just under the timeline.
  • Item rows — packed items with category chips, reuse pills, and delete.
  • Suggestion chips — dress-code essentials, dashed border to differentiate from packed items.
