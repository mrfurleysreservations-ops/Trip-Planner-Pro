Concept C+F — modal picker with full-page visualization

Same full-page layout as Concept F (header, sub-nav, person tabs, view tabs, step indicator, slot card, reuse strip, sticky Prev/Next CTA), but tapping a slot opens a Concept-C bottom-sheet modal. The modal knows which slot you tapped, so there's no category picker or auto-routing — tap Top, you get tops. No Head slot: headwear (hats, beanies) lives inside Accessories. No smart input at the card level — you add items through the sheet.

What changed vs. Concept F

Removed: Head slot

Hats, beanies, caps, visors live inside Accessories. Keeps the 6-tile grid tight and matches how people think about hats — an accessory, not a top.

Removed: Smart auto-type input

No floor-of-card input. No auto-routing guesswork. The slot you tap is the slot the item lands in.

Kept: Bottom-sheet modal (Concept C)

Modal stays consistent with the app's other "add thing" flows. Reuse chips → dress-code suggestions → keyboard input, in priority order.

Kept: Dress slot for women

Replaces where Head used to live. Pink-tinted to signal it's a "whole outfit" choice — filling it dims/hides Top & Bottom (design TBD).
