How "Groups · Outfits · Pack-and-Go" work together

  • Person chip row is the group filter. All shows every household member stacked (host/parent view — good for coordinating kids). Tap one person to see just their stuff.
  • Outfits live inside each event card in By-Event view. One outfit per event per person (matches packing_outfits table). A piece marked orange = missing from their suitcase and will auto-add to Master List.
  • Items (non-outfit gear — sunscreen, reservation, floaties) sit below the outfits in the event card, colour-tagged by owner. "SHARED" items count once, not per-person.
  • Master List is the auto-consolidated suitcase for the selected person. Each item shows which event(s) it came from, so nothing gets packed "just in case" — every item has a reason.
  • Pack-and-Go = the checkbox. Check it when the item is in the bag. The progress card ("11 of 19") rolls up live. On travel day you can switch to a "Going today" filter (future enhancement) to see only items needed before leaving the house.

Host / family-member permissions

  • Host (Joe) can view and edit any member's packing — essential for packing a kid's bag. That's why the chip row includes everyone by default.
  • Adult members (Sarah) see their own packing + shared items. They can optionally view others' if the host enables it.
  • Kids (Mike, Emma) don't have their own logins — their packing is managed by a parent. The 🧒 icon in the chip flags "this person is a dependent."
  • Opt-in is per event (event_participants) — if Sarah opts out of hiking, her outfit block doesn't appear on that event card and trail-shoes won't land in her Master List.
