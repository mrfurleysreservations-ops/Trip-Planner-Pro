Onboarding Mockup v2

Updated: Sticky bottom nav + packing step + bigger everything

🔑 Big Structural Change: Sticky Bottom Navigation

Current: Back/Next buttons are inline at the bottom of the content — users must scroll past everything to find them. On tall steps (Style, Packing), the buttons disappear below the fold.

Proposed: Back/Next buttons are pinned to the bottom of the screen in a fixed bar — always visible, always under the user's thumb. The content area scrolls independently between the progress dots and the nav bar. This is the standard pattern in every major mobile app's onboarding flow (Apple, Airbnb, Duolingo, etc.).

Bonus: This pattern can be rolled out app-wide for any page with a primary action (Save Trip, Add Item, Confirm, etc.).
